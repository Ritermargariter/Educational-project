Monaco Race Week 2020 regatta results

Over a hundred participants gathered on the coast of Monaco to compete for the main prize. The competition was attended by both beginners and professional sailors with considerable experience behind them.

Throughout the voyage, we were accompanied by the sun, beautiful weather, on some days the wind was up to 20 knots. The regatta was well organized, 135 yachtsmen, 7 crews, 9 participating countries, 4 racing days, 3 divisions.

Participating teams:

  • Louise
  • Fortune Sailing
  • Anna Maria
  • Leviathan
  • Black Swan
  • White Pepper
  • Pearl

The Fortune Sailing team became the champion of the Monaco Race Week 2020 regatta, last year stopping one step away from victory, and this season ahead of its closest rival, the Louise crew, by 12 points 5 points.

We are very friendly in the team and understand each other without words, I think that's why we won

Congratulations to the winners and we look forward to seeing all the participants again at the autumn regattas!

Adam Joly 20.06.2021
